Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What kind of services do you provide?
A: We offer both interior and exterior remodeling and upgrading for residential and commercial properties.

Q: Do you provide the team of workers?
A: No, but we do coordinate the completion of the project with a contractor.

Q: Do you pick and choose furniture, appliances and fabrics?
A: Yes. We also have services for shades, flooring, etc.

Q: How do you charge?
A: Hourly or on a cost plus basis, depending on the size of the project.

Q: Will you use my original items?
A: Due to the present economic situation, we will reuse the clients furniture and household items to fit the new design and setting.
